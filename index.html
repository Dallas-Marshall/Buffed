Meet Our Trainer

Matt Carriker

Matt has over 10 years experience in the health and wellness industry. He prides himself on being approachable, relatable and empathetic; the perfect mix for your next personal trainer. Matt specialises in weight loss and muscle gain but has experience in all aspects of personal training.

Matt founded Buffed Personal Training back in 2019, after noticing a lack of affordable yet quality personal training services in the Townsville region.

Since it's founding Buffed Personal Training has helped hundreds of clients reach their goals.
